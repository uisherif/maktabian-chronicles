# Plot Overview

## Story Arc

### Beginning (Chapters 1-2)
- Introduce protagonist and their ordinary world
- Establish the central conflict or inciting incident
- Set up stakes and motivations

### Middle (Chapters 3-4)
- Escalate the conflict
- Develop character relationships
- Present obstacles and challenges
- Midpoint revelation or turning point

### End (Chapter 5)
- Climax and resolution
- Character transformation
- Tie up loose ends
- Deliver the story's message or theme
